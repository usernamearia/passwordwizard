Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a password secure?

A secure password should be long (at least 12 characters), include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters, and be unique for each account. Avoid using personal information or common words.

How does the breach check work?

We use the Have I Been Pwned API which checks if your password has appeared in known data breaches. We only send a partial hash of your password, so your full password never leaves your device.

How can I remember complex passwords?

We recommend using a password manager to securely store your complex passwords. This way, you only need to remember one master password while maintaining unique, strong passwords for all your accounts.

Yes! WizardPass is completely free to use. We believe everyone deserves access to strong password security.
